**AI for Construction | BIM Automation | XR | Robotics | Drone Training**

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Civil Engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington. My research lies at the intersection of construction engineering, artificial intelligence, robotics, and immersive technologies. I work on AI-assisted conversion of hand-drawn floor plans into BIM, XR-based training systems for drone operation and FAA compliance, multimodal AI for construction safety, and human-robot interaction for construction applications. My broader goal is to develop practical digital tools that make infrastructure and construction workflows safer, more efficient, and more accessible through automation, intelligent assistance, and technology-enabled training.
